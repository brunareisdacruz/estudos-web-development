The Best Movies According to Bruna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fallen Angels (1995)

The night’s full of weirdos.

An assassin goes through obstacles as he attempts to escape his violent lifestyle despite the opposition of his partner, who is secretly attracted to him.

Monster (2023)

Are they the ones we dream of, or the ones we fail to see among us?

After an outburst at school involving her son, a concerned single mother demands answers, triggering a sequence of deepening suspicion and turmoil.

All About Lily Chou-Chou (2001)

Pain can take you in prison. The ether can set you free.

Charts the troubled teenage years of students Yūichi Hasumi and Shūsuke Hoshino, exploring the shifting and complex power dynamics of their relationship against the backdrop of Yūichi’s love for the dreamy and abstract music of pop star Lily Chou-Chou.
